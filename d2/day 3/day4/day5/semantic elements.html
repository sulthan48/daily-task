Introduction to Geopolitics

Geopolitics is the study of how geography, economics, politics, and international relations influence the decisions of countries.

Nations work together through diplomacy, trade, security alliances, and international organizations to maintain peace and protect their interests.

Why Geopolitics Matters

Geopolitics affects global trade, energy resources, national security, climate cooperation, and economic growth.

Understanding geopolitics helps people learn how world events influence everyday life.
